About SYED JUNAID AHMED!

Hey ! I am SYED JUNAID AHMED. I'm from Andhra Pradesh, which is one of the largest coastal region in India. I competed my graduation in Mechanical Engineering in VSM COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING and have also worked as Research Assistant there for a period of one year . During which, I taught students about how to think and develop their project ideas. I introduced them to the DIY(Do IT YourSelf) culture by teaching them Arduino.

I love to travel for conferences and participate in the Hack-a-thons and Build-a-thons. I have participated in MIT-LVPEI Health workshop in Hyderabad and Recently particpated in S.T.E.A.M School in collaboration with MakersAsylum and the French Embassy of India. And my last tour was the memorable Fab Conference is FAN3(Fablab Asia Network) which was a Week Long taking place at three places (Mumbai-Pune-Kochi)

I am very interested to make things ,so I have started a maker-space in my home town named Maker's Hive, which I want to scale up. For that purpose i am here. I also have plans to join the proposed Fablab at Vizag Aka Vishakapatnam once it starts ,learning everything perfectly, and working around the clock to graduate !

please find my personal website at junaid.tech
